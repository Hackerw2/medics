About HealthVision

HealthVision is a revolutionary healthcare application designed to make medical diagnosis and healthcare access easier than ever before. Our cutting-edge AI technology helps you identify potential health issues quickly and connects you with the right medical professionals.

Whether you're experiencing symptoms or have noticed something unusual, HealthVision provides preliminary insights and guidance to help you take the next steps in your healthcare journey.

Our Key Features

Symptom & Image Input

Manually enter symptoms or upload images of the affected area for instant AI-powered analysis.

AI Disease Detection

Our system identifies potential diseases based on your symptoms or uploaded images with smart accuracy.

Nearby Hospitals

Get a list of nearby hospitals using your current location and connect with the nearest professionals.

Report Summarization

Upload medical reports and receive a simplified summary that highlights key information and advice.

Appointment Scheduler

Book appointments with doctors, sync them to your calendar, and receive timely reminders & alerts.
